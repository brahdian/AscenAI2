# Billing & Usage

Understand your plan, track your usage, and manage your subscription.

---

## Understanding Your Plan

AscenAI2 offers plans designed to grow with your business. Each plan includes a set of features and usage limits.

### Plan Overview

**Starter Plan**
- 1 AI agent
- 500 chat conversations per month
- 60 voice minutes per month
- Basic tools (email, SMS)
- Email support
- Best for: Solo businesses just getting started

**Growth Plan**
- 3 AI agents
- 2,000 chat conversations per month
- 300 voice minutes per month
- All tools (calendar, payments, webhooks)
- Priority email support
- Best for: Growing businesses with multiple needs

**Scale Plan**
- 10 AI agents
- 10,000 chat conversations per month
- 1,000 voice minutes per month
- All tools plus custom API integrations
- Priority support with dedicated account manager
- Best for: Busy operations with high customer volume

### What Counts as a Conversation?

A conversation is a single chat session with your agent. It starts when a customer opens the chat widget and sends their first message, and ends after 30 minutes of inactivity.

**Examples:**
- A customer asks 5 questions in one chat session = 1 conversation
- A customer chats in the morning, then comes back in the afternoon = 2 conversations
- A customer opens the widget but doesn't send a message = 0 conversations

### What Counts as a Voice Minute?

Voice minutes are the total duration of all phone calls handled by your agent.

**Examples:**
- A 3-minute call = 3 voice minutes
- Ten 2-minute calls = 20 voice minutes
- A call that lasts 1 minute and 45 seconds = 2 voice minutes (rounded up)

## Viewing Your Usage

### Usage Dashboard

1. In your dashboard, click **"Billing"** in the left sidebar
2. Click the **"Usage"** tab
3. You'll see a visual overview of your current usage

### What You'll See

**Chat Conversations**
- A progress bar showing how many conversations you've used vs. your limit
- A breakdown by day so you can see your busiest times
- A projection of your end-of-month usage based on current trends

**Voice Minutes**
- A progress bar showing how many voice minutes you've used vs. your limit
- A list of recent calls with duration and date
- Total voice minutes used this month

**Agents**
- How many agents you've created vs. your plan limit
- Status of each agent (active, paused, draft)

**Tool Usage**
- Number of emails sent
- Number of SMS messages sent
- Number of calendar events booked
- Number of payments processed

### Usage Alerts

AscenAI2 will notify you when you're approaching your limits:

- **80% usage** - Email notification warning you're getting close
- **90% usage** - Email notification with upgrade options
- **100% usage** - Email notification and banner in your dashboard

> **Tip:** Don't wait until you hit your limit. If you consistently use 80% or more of your plan, consider upgrading before you run out.

## Upgrading Your Plan

### When to Upgrade

Consider upgrading if:
- You're consistently hitting 80%+ of your conversation limit
- You're running out of voice minutes
- You need more agents (e.g., one for bookings, one for support)
- You need tools not available on your current plan
- Your business is growing and you expect more customer interactions

### How to Upgrade

1. Go to **Billing** > **Plan**
2. Click **"Upgrade Plan"**
3. Choose your new plan
4. Review the price difference
5. Click **"Confirm Upgrade"**

**What happens when you upgrade:**
- Your new limits take effect immediately
- You're charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle
- Your next full billing cycle will be at the new plan's price
- All your existing agents, playbooks, and settings are preserved

### Downgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Billing** > **Plan**
2. Click **"Change Plan"**
3. Choose a lower plan
4. Review the changes
5. Click **"Confirm Downgrade"**

**What happens when you downgrade:**
- The change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle
- If you have more agents than the lower plan allows, you'll need to pause or delete the extras before the downgrade takes effect
- If you exceed the lower plan's conversation limit, additional conversations may be charged at a per-conversation rate or paused (depending on your settings)

## Payment Methods

### Accepted Payment Methods

- Visa credit and debit cards
- Mastercard credit and debit cards
- American Express
- Discover

### Adding a Payment Method

1. Go to **Billing** > **Payment Methods**
2. Click **"Add Payment Method"**
3. Enter your card details
4. Click **"Save"**
5. The new card becomes your default payment method

### Updating Your Payment Method

If your card is expiring or you want to use a different card:

1. Go to **Billing** > **Payment Methods**
2. Click **"Add Payment Method"** and enter the new card
3. Click **"Set as Default"** next to the new card
4. You can remove the old card after the new one is set as default

### Failed Payments

If a payment fails:

1. You'll receive an email notification
2. AscenAI2 will retry the payment after 3 days
3. If the second attempt fails, you'll receive another email
4. After 7 days without successful payment, your account may be paused

**To resolve a failed payment:**
1. Go to **Billing** > **Payment Methods**
2. Update your card information
3. Click **"Retry Payment"**
4. Your account will be reactivated immediately upon successful payment

## Understanding Your Invoice

### Where to Find Invoices

1. Go to **Billing** > **Invoices**
2. You'll see a list of all past invoices
3. Click any invoice to view or download it as a PDF

### What's on Your Invoice

- **Plan cost** - Your monthly subscription fee
- **Overage charges** - Any conversations or minutes beyond your plan limit
- **Add-ons** - Any additional features you've purchased
- **Tax** - Applicable taxes based on your location
- **Total** - The total amount charged

### Billing Cycle

- Your billing cycle starts on the day you subscribed
- You're billed monthly on the same date
- Your first bill may be prorated if you signed up mid-cycle

### Cancelling Your Subscription

1. Go to **Billing** > **Plan**
2. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Choose a reason for cancelling (optional)
4. Click **"Confirm Cancellation"**

**What happens when you cancel:**
- Your account remains active until the end of your current billing period
- You won't be charged again
- All your agents, playbooks, and data are preserved for 30 days after cancellation
- You can reactivate your account at any time within 30 days
- After 30 days, your data may be deleted

> **Tip:** If you're cancelling because of cost, contact our support team. We may be able to offer a plan that better fits your budget.

## Managing Costs

### Tips to Stay Within Your Plan

**Reduce unnecessary conversations:**
- Make sure your agent's greeting is clear so customers know what it can help with
- Add a "quick answers" section to your website for common questions
- Use the agent on specific pages where customers need help most

**Optimize voice usage:**
- Set up after-hours messages to avoid unnecessary calls
- Use call transfer wisely so the agent doesn't handle calls better suited for humans
- Review call recordings to identify conversations that could be shorter

**Monitor regularly:**
- Check your usage dashboard weekly
- Set up usage alerts (they're on by default)
- Review chat history to see if conversations are efficient

### Understanding Overage Charges

If you exceed your plan limits, you may be charged for additional usage:

- **Extra conversations** - Charged per conversation beyond your limit
- **Extra voice minutes** - Charged per minute beyond your limit

Overage rates vary by plan. Check your plan details in **Billing** > **Plan** for specific rates.

### Setting Usage Limits

You can set hard limits to prevent unexpected charges:

1. Go to **Billing** > **Usage Limits**
2. Toggle **"Stop at limit"** for conversations or voice minutes
3. When the limit is reached, your agent will stop accepting new conversations or calls until the next billing cycle

> **Note:** If you enable hard limits, customers won't be able to chat with or call your agent once you hit your limit. Consider upgrading instead if you expect consistent growth.

## Common Billing Questions

### "Why did my bill change this month?"

Possible reasons:
- You upgraded or downgraded your plan
- You had overage charges for exceeding your limits
- You added or removed team members (if billed per seat)
- Tax rates changed based on your location

### "Can I get a refund?"

Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Contact our support team at **billing@ascenai.com** with your account details and reason for the refund request.

### "Do unused conversations roll over?"

No. Unused conversations and voice minutes reset each billing cycle. They do not roll over to the next month.

### "Can I pause my account?"

Yes. You can pause your account for up to 3 months:
1. Go to **Billing** > **Plan**
2. Click **"Pause Account"**
3. Choose how long to pause (1, 2, or 3 months)
4. Your account will automatically reactivate after the pause period

During the pause:
- Your agents are paused and won't respond to customers
- You won't be charged
- Your data is preserved
- You can reactivate early if needed

### "What happens if I exceed my limit?"

You have two options:
1. **Soft limit (default)** - You continue to be served but are charged for overage
2. **Hard limit** - Your agent stops responding until the next billing cycle

You can change this setting in **Billing** > **Usage Limits**.
